Devops Team Roadmap-Campus Hire

The trainer is scheduled to meet all the candidates in person for one hour every Monday at Parel Office for a training session. Subsequent to this session, participants are expected to engage in practice activities assigned by the trainer for the duration of one hour every day from Tuesday to Friday of that week. In the next training session, the trainer will conduct an evaluation, recording each candidate's scores & detailed observations, transforming it into a feedback session with participants.
